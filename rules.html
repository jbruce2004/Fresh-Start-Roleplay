Server Rules

1. Threats & Harassment

No threatening people outside of RolePlay. This includes DDOS Threats, DOX Threats, Death Threats, Modding Threats and telling someone to "Kill yourself (KYS)".

Punishment: Permanent Ban in Game & Discord

2. Player Disputes

No arguing with other players outside of RolePlay. Always be respectful and courteous. If you have an issue, try to resolve it yourselves or contact staff via /calladmin.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Warning, 3rd: Kick

3. Deathmatch Rules

No Random Deathmatch (RDM): Shooting someone without any roleplay.
No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM): Running over someone without reason.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Ban

4. Hate Speech

No Homophobic/Racial Slurs: F slurs and hate speech towards LGBTQ+ community or any racial slurs in any language will not be tolerated.

Punishment: Ban

5. Modding & Cheating

No Modding: Modding the server or attempting to bypass the anticheat.

Punishment: Permanent Ban in Game & Discord

6. Respect & Spam

No Player or Staff Diss
No Mass Spawning Vehicles
No Spamming Chat

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Ban

7. Staff Interaction

No Arguing with Staff: Staff have final say in all situations. If you disagree, use the staff report ticket in Discord.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Ban

8. Roleplay Standards

No Fail RolePlay: Uphold your character's value of life. No unrealistic actions or self-reviving during active scenes.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Ban

9. Forced Roleplay

No Forcing RolePlay onto Another Player: If someone declines roleplay, respect their decision.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Ban

10. Meta-Gaming & NLR

No Meta-Gaming: Using outside game information for advantage.
No Breaking New Life Rule (NLR): No memory of events leading to death, 15-minute scene cooldown.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Ban

11. Emergency Vehicles

No Stealing Emergency Vehicles: RolePlay involving stealing emergency vehicles is not allowed.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Ban

12. Death Communication

No Talking while Dead: If you need to /respawn or /revive, you are dead and should not communicate.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Warning, 3rd: Kick

13. Sexual Roleplay

Sexual roleplay is allowed with consent and ERP Certified Role. No pedophilia or bestiality. Animal on Animal is allowed.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Temp Ban, 4th: Perm Ban

14. Realistic Driving

No GTA Driving or Unrealistic Driving: Staff have final say on what is realistic. Examples: 300 mph driving, reckless driving, off-road in lowriders.

Punishment: 1st: Warning, 2nd: Kick, 3rd: Temporary Ban
